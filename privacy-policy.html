Privacy Policy

Football Live Wallpaper

Introduction

Welcome to Football Live Wallpaper. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information associated with your use of our application. This Privacy Policy details our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Live Wallpaper service.

1. Information Collection and Use

Football Live Wallpaper does not collect, store, upload, or share any personal data or information.

The app operates completely offline. It runs as an interactive 3D physics-based simulation on your device's home screen. We do not have any servers, nor do we use any third-party analytics or advertising networks that collect your information.

2. Device Permissions

The app requires the standard permission to set live wallpapers (android.software.live_wallpaper). This permission is used solely to allow the Android operating system to set and display the live wallpaper on your screen. No other sensitive device permissions (such as location, camera, microphone, or storage access) are requested or used.

3. Children's Privacy

Since our application does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR regulations regarding children's privacy.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by email at your developer support email address.
